Volcanoes and Archaeological Treasures

Walking on Giants

Santa Ana Volcano

Location: Department of Santa Ana, part of the Apaneca Mountain Range.

Date of last eruption: October 1, 2005.

Description: The tallest volcano in El Salvador, known for its turquoise crater lake.

San Salvador Volcano

Location: Department of San Salvador, located in San Salvador.

Date of last eruption: 1917

Description: El Boquerón National Park is located in the crater of the San Salvador volcano; it is a protected area with a great diversity of fauna and flora.

Mayan Archaeological Sites

Joya de Cerén

Location: Department of La Libertad.

Date of 'inauguration' as a modern site (discovery): 1976.

Description: Called the "Pompeii of America", a Mayan town that was preserved under volcanic ash.
