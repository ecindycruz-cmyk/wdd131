Nombre del Sitio

Hidden Places of El Salvador: Unforgettable Sites

Description and Justification

The name follows the reference design model, using "Hidden Places of El Salvador: Unforgettable Sites" to denote both travel itineraries and tourist destinations.

Logo (Example Description)

A design that merges a stylized wave with a minimalist mountain, using navy blue and emerald green. The logo represents the duality of the region (sea and mountains).

Optional Domain Availability

lugares-turisticos.com (Hypothetical)

Site Purpose

The main purpose of "Hidden Places of El Salvador: Unforgettable Sites" is to be the go-to digital guide for planning trips to the region. The site will provide:

  • Predefined Tourist Sites (Sun and Beach, Culture and Towns, Adventure).
  • Detailed information on attractions, gastronomy, and accommodations.
  • A Blog with travel tips and interviews with locals.
  • A Contact form to request personalized guided tours.

The goal is to inspire and facilitate the planning of a memorable trip to the Costa Verde.

Scenarios

Questions that represent visitors and guide the creation of site content, "Why visit El Salvador?"

  • What is the best time of year to surf on the Costa Verde and where can I rent equipment nearby? (Audience: Adventure and Beach Tourists).
  • We are planning a 5-day family trip. Do you have a recommended itinerary that combines historical sites with a relaxing day at the beach? (Audience: Travelers planning long trips).
  • Where can I find information about culinary traditions and local markets in the towns of El Salvador? (Audience: Tourists interested in Culture and Gastronomy).
