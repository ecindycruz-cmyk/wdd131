Beach Route: Sun, Sand and Surf

Our Must-See Beaches

El Tunco

Location:La Libertad, Costa del Bálsamo.

Opening as a major surfing destination: 1970s (Massive popularity: 2000s).

Description: Famous for its pig-shaped rock and consistent waves, ideal for surfers and nightlife.

Las Flores

Location: It is located in the department of San Miguel.

This point produces waves up to 12 feet (4 meters) high, with long, steep lines.

Description: It is known as "The Wild East" because of its nature, but also its remoteness.

La Libertad

Location:La Libertad is one of the departments, located in the central zone,

You'll be enjoying a feast of fresh seafood, disconnecting from the hustle and bustle of the city, and taking in a beautiful sunset.

Description:La Libertad is becoming an ideal department for tourism, where visitors can choose from a wide range of hotels, from the most demanding to the simplest, but all with good service.
